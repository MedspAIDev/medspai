The missed call problem

Why med spas miss calls

Missed calls happen most when your team is busy.

  • During treatments, when no one can pick up
  • During peak hours, when calls stack up
  • After hours and on weekends, when the desk is closed
  • When staff is juggling check-ins and consults

Each missed call can be a lost consult.

Lost consults mean lost revenue.

Financial impact

Why missed calls cost money

Missed calls are a leak. The cost grows fast.

  • Consults do not get booked, so treatment revenue is lost
  • Paid ads bring calls that vanish, wasting ad spend
  • Callers who get no answer try another med spa
  • You rarely get a second chance with a high-intent caller

Why fixes fail

Why common fixes fail

Most fixes still let calls slip away. This is true even with modern phone answering alternatives.

Voicemail

Voicemail asks people to wait. Many hang up and leave no details.

Callbacks

Callbacks are slow. By then, the caller may book elsewhere.

Front desk-only coverage

Front desks juggle check-ins, phones, and walk-ins. They still miss calls.

Traditional answering services

Scripted services miss key details and can’t always book correctly.
