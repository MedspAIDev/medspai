Getting Leads Is Not Your Problem. Converting Them Is.

Most med spas think they need more traffic, more followers, more ads. But more leads only help if you’re actually converting the ones you already get.

Let’s say your website gets 500 visitors a month. Out of those, maybe 40 message you or fill out a form. If you book 10 of them, you’re converting 25 percent. That leaves 30 warm leads on the table.

That is the gap. And that is where most revenue is lost.
