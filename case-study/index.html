How One Med Spa Booked $23,280 in 60 Days Without Spending a Dime on Ads

Peak Health & Aesthetics, a busy med spa in Cypress, Texas, became the first clinic to adopt our custom-built AI assistant. The early results helped shape the foundation of MedspAI.

Background

Like many growing med spas, Peak Health & Aesthetics had a common challenge. Inquiries were coming in after hours, staff were stretched thin, and follow-up often took too long. This led to missed opportunities and lost revenue.

The Challenge

Peak was not running paid ads. All leads were organic, mostly from Google searches and social referrals. Even with steady traffic, too many prospects never made it to the calendar because no one followed up in time.

The Solution

A fully customized AI assistant was deployed to respond to inquiries instantly, answer common treatment questions, qualify leads, and book consultations directly into the calendar. The assistant worked 24/7, including after hours when most conversions were being lost.
