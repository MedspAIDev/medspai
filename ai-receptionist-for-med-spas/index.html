AI Receptionist for Med Spas

An AI receptionist for med spas answers inbound calls, captures patient details, and books consultations without sending callers to voicemail. It solves missed calls during busy hours and after hours by giving every caller a real-time response.

Why med spas need an AI receptionist

Med spas run into the same daily issues: phones ring during treatments, after-hours calls go unanswered, and staff cannot keep up with every inquiry. These missed calls and delays lead to lost consults and revenue, plus a front desk that is forced to multitask instead of focusing on in-person guests.

  • Missed calls during busy hours
  • Missed calls after hours
  • Lost revenue from unanswered inquiries
  • Front desk overload

Why traditional solutions fail

Voicemail, human answering services, and front desk staff alone all leave gaps for med spas. Voicemail depends on callers leaving a message, which many do not. Human answering services are generic and often lack med spa context. Front desk staff cannot answer every call while running the clinic.

  • Voicemail collects messages but does not qualify or book.
  • Human answering services follow scripts but are not built for med spa scheduling.
  • Front desk staff alone cannot cover peak times and after hours.
